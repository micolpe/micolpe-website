Quick Guide Getting Started with Micolpe

Follow these steps to install the application, create your account, manage your loft, add your pigeons and use Micolpe's pigeon-racing tools.

Download the app Need help?
1
⬇️

Download the app

Download and setup the Micolpe application on Desktop or Android by following the instructions.

2
👤

Create your account

Register and confirm your subscription via email.

3
🏠

Complete the subscription

Log in, complete your loft details, choose your language and configure the appearance of your pedigrees.

4
🐦

Add yours pigeons

Start adding your pigeons using different options: record, writing pedigree, adding siblings, importing .W50 files, ..

5
🌳

Write and scan your pedigrees

Build your pigeons' pedigrees from your existing PDF documents or images, without unnecessary manual re-entry.

6
📄

Export in PDF

Preview the pedigree in A4 format and then share or download the file.

7
🤖

Generate AI Posters

Import photos of your champions or breeders and automatically generate professional posters with artificial intelligence.

8
🏁

Use the racing tools

Calculate the distance, speed, performance and statistics of your pigeons in races.

9
☁️

Offline work

Continue using the application without a connection, then sync your data when you reconnect to the internet.
